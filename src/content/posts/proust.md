title: Modified Proust Questionnaire
slug: proust
date: 2024-07
status: public
---

1. What is your idea of perfect happiness?
2. What is your greatest fear?
3. What is the trait you most deplore in yourself?
4. What is the trait you most deplore in others?
5. Which living person do you most admire?
6. What is your greatest extravagance?
7. What do you consider the most overrated virtue?
8. On what occasion do you lie?
9. Which living person do you most despise?
10. What is the quality you most like in another person?
11. Which words or phrases do you most overuse?
12. When and where were you happiest?
13. Which talent would you most like to have?
14. If you could change one thing about yourself, what would it be?
15. What do you consider your greatest achievement?
16. Where would you most like to live?
17. What is your most treasured possession?
18. What do you regard as the lowest depth of misery?
19. What do you most value in your friends?
20. Who are your heros?
21. What is your greatest regret?
22. How would you like to die?
23. What is your motto?
